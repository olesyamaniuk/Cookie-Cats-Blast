Blast Cookies, Solve Puzzles, and Save the Kittens!

About the Game

Join the lovable Cookie Cats in their latest adventure! Cookie Cats Blast is a delightful match-3 puzzle game where you’ll solve exciting puzzles, blast cookies, and help save the kittens from tricky challenges. Travel through vibrant neighborhoods, uncover fun surprises, and meet quirky cat characters in this pawsome adventure!

Features

  • Exciting Match-3 Gameplay:

    Blast colorful cookies in hundreds of levels filled with fun and challenging puzzles.

  • Adorable Cat Companions:

    Meet a variety of quirky and lovable cats, each with their own unique personality.

  • Creative Power-ups:

    Use explosive power-ups and clever boosters to overcome tricky puzzles and obstacles.

  • Interactive Map:

    Travel through a colorful world filled with cat-themed neighborhoods, parks, and hidden treasures.

  • Compete with Friends:

    Connect with friends to see who can achieve the highest scores and fastest progress.

  • Offline Mode:

    Play anytime, anywhere, even without an internet connection.

How to Play

  • Match & Blast:

    Tap groups of 3 or more matching cookies to blast them off the board and earn points.

  • Use Power-ups:

    Combine special cookies to create powerful boosters that can help clear difficult levels.

  • Save the Kittens:

    Complete level objectives such as rescuing kittens or reaching specific scores.

  • Complete Levels:

    Solve puzzles by clearing the board, gathering stars, and unlocking new levels.

  • Advance Through the Map:

    As you progress, unlock new neighborhoods and areas filled with even more challenging puzzles and adorable kittens.
